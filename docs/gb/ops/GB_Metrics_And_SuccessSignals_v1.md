# GroupBuild Metrics and Success Signals v1

Metrics should serve learning, not vanity.

## Participation metrics

- Applications per circle.
- Accepted participants per circle.
- Attendance rate per session.
- Drop-off rate across the journey.


## Engagement metrics

- Experiments committed vs completed.
- Number of peer interactions per participant (for example, comments, check-ins).
- Use of AI tools within and between sessions (self-reported or observed).


## Outcome metrics

- Self-reported clarity about direction before vs after the journey.
- Self-reported sense of agency and support before vs after.
- Concrete outcomes, where applicable:
  - Applications submitted.
  - Projects started.
  - Roles changed or created.


## Qualitative signals

- Stories participants are willing to share publicly (even if anonymized).
- Testimonials about the circle experience.
- Feedback from community partners, sponsors, or employers.


## Health of the circles

- Do participants feel psychologically safe?
- Is facilitation sustainable for hosts?
- Does the circle format feel repeatable without burning people out?
