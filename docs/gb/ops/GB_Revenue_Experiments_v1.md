# GroupBuild Revenue Experiments v1

This document lists possible revenue experiments to test after initial pilots.

## Experiment 1 — Sliding-scale contributions

- Offer a suggested range (for example, $25–$200 for a 6-week circle).
- Allow people to pay less or more.
- Track:
  - Uptake.
  - Participant comfort.
  - Whether contributions correlate with engagement.


## Experiment 2 — Sponsored seats

- Approach a union, association, or employer to fund seats.
- Package:
  - A defined cohort (e.g., 2 circles of 8 participants).
  - A short report with anonymized outcomes.
- Test willingness to fund follow-on cohorts.


## Experiment 3 — Host training

- Offer a short training program for facilitators who want to run circles in their own communities.
- Charge a modest fee and/or partner fee.
- Provide:
  - Templates.
  - Facilitation tips.
  - Optional co-hosting in a first circle.


## Experiment 4 — Organizational cohorts

- Run internal circles for a specific organization.
- Focus on AI-era strategy and workforce transitions.
- Charge at a level more typical of B2B engagements.
