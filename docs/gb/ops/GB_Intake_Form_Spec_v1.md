# GroupBuild Intake Form Spec v1

This spec can be implemented with any form tool.

## Fields

- Name or pseudonym (short text)
- Email or preferred contact channel (short text)
- Region / time zone (short text or dropdown)
- Primary way AI is affecting your work or plans (long text)
- What you hope a circle would help you do (long text)
- Role interest (checkboxes):
  - Join a circle
  - Host a circle
  - Sponsor seats or circles
- Availability (multi-select, days and rough time bands)
- Comfort with using AI tools (scale or multiple choice)
- Anything else we should know (optional long text)


## Matching hints

- Try to group people by overlapping availability and rough sector.
- Avoid circles where one participant is an obvious employer of another.
- Consider mixing levels of AI comfort, but avoid extreme gaps.
