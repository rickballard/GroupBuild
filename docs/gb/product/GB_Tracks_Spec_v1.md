# GroupBuild Tracks Spec v1

This expands the three-track concept into implementable outlines.

## Track: Reinvent Work

- Audience: individuals whose work is being reshaped by AI.
- Format: default circle (6–10 people, 6–8 weeks).
- Inputs:
  - Intake form capturing role, sector, main worries, constraints.
  - Simple self-assessment of skills and interests.
- Outputs:
  - 3–5 documented experiments per participant.
  - A short reflection on "What I know now that I did not know when we started."
  - Optional story for public sharing (anonymized if needed).


## Track: Build a Cause (future)

- Audience: small groups planning campaigns, mutual aid projects, or civic interventions.
- Format: circle, but with a shared project at the center.
- Inputs:
  - Basic description of the cause and context.
  - Rough list of constraints and risks.
  - Clarity on who is directly affected by the issue.
- Outputs:
  - Draft narrative: why this cause, why now.
  - Simple plan: first actions over 4–8 weeks.
  - Early experiments: message tests, ally outreach, support systems.


## Track: Ship a Side Project (future)

- Audience: people wanting to move a project idea closer to first users.
- Format: circle focused on product experiments.
- Inputs:
  - Project idea written in 1–2 paragraphs.
  - Constraints: time, money, skills.
- Outputs:
  - A minimal scope for the first iteration.
  - User research conversations or tests.
  - A decision at the end: continue, pivot, pause, or hand off.
